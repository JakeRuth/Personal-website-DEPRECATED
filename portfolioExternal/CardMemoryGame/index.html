How to play

  • Read the instructions that appear beneath the title at the top of the page.
  • The instructions will change from round to round.
  • Remember the cards on the screen, in the order specified at the top of the page.
  • When the cards turn over and the response selection appears, select the cards in the order that was specified above (either 'left to right', or 'right to left').
